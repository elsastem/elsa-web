Privacy Statement

University of Canberra and the Department respect the privacy of individuals, protect personal information and comply with relevant Australian privacy laws. University of Canberra and the Department will collect personal information from you when you complete this application form. We will use your personal information only for the purposes for which it has been collected. The purposes include assessing your preschool’s application to participate in the ELSA program, and, if the application is successful, setting up your preschool to begin the program.

If you believe that the personal information that we hold about you is incorrect, please contact us at contact@elsa.edu.au and reasonable steps will be taken to amend that information.
