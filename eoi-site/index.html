About the Program

Early Learning STEM Australia (ELSA) is a play-based digital learning program for children in preschool. To explore Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths (STEM).

Digitally Assisted

ELSA apps will go beyond the screen to encourage active play that supports STEM practices. Our apps support learning through play, and act as a springboard for children to explore the natural world as they play with sand, leaves, blocks and water.

Pilot Study

This new program is being developed with educators in 2017, and will be rolled out to 100 centres nationally for a pilot program in 2018. Express your interest in the pilot before 10 April for a chance to be involved with this innovative learning program.

What is STEM?

Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths.

STEM is an integrated approach to teaching science, technology, engineering and maths by exploring the real world.

You already use STEM at your preschool when you give children opportunities to:

  • Create a community garden
  • Build structures that are strong or tall
  • Fill, pour and move sand or water
  • Collect different kinds of leaves or seeds
  • Create patterns or draw designs

When we talk about STEM in preschool, we tend to think about STEM practices such as exploring place and patterns, problem solving, designing, making, and spatial reasoning.

Exploring STEM principles helps children weave strong skill ropes that can be used in many ways in later life. They can learn about trial and error, critical thinking, and the environment.

Pilot Program

Help us forge Australia's STEM learning stratergies.

  • Starting in 2018

    A hundred centres across Australia will take part in the ELSA Pilot. These centres will be the first to try the ELSA apps, and receive free workshops and resources about incorporating STEM into their preschool program.

  • Applying is easy

    Simply send in details about your centre, and we will let you know if you are successful. We are looking for a diverse range of centres, to mimic the diversity found across Australia. So whether you are urban, regional or remote, large or small, government, community, independent, Catholic, or anything else, we want to include you.

  • Eligibility

    The only thing you need is a tablet device, such as an iPad, that your preschool children can play with. If you don’t have one yet, you can commit to purchasing one before October if you are selected. There is no cost for ELSA, but ELSA does not provide funding for devices.
